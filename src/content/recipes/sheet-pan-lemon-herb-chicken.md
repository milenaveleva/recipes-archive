1. Heat the oven to 200 °C fan (220 °C conventional). Bring a pan of salted water to the boil and parboil the **baby potatoes** for 8 minutes, then drain.
2. In a large bowl, toss the **chicken thighs**, parboiled potatoes and **red onion** with the **olive oil**, **garlic**, **oregano**, the **zest and juice of one lemon**, and a good pinch of salt and pepper.
3. Spread everything in a single layer on a large baking tray. Roast for 20 minutes.
4. Add the **broccoli florets** to the tray, turning to coat in the pan juices. Roast for a further 12–15 minutes until the chicken is cooked through and the potatoes are golden.
5. Squeeze over the **remaining lemon** and serve straight from the tray.

## Notes

- Cut the potatoes no larger than 3 cm so they finish at the same time as the chicken.
- Swap broccoli for green beans or tenderstem; add them at the same later stage so they keep some bite.
