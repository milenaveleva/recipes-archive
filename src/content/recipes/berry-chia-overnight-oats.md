1. In a jar or bowl, stir together the **rolled oats**, **chia seeds**, **milk**, **Greek yogurt** and **maple syrup** until evenly combined.
2. Cover and refrigerate overnight, or at least 4 hours, until thick and creamy.
3. In the morning, loosen with a splash of milk if needed, then top with **mixed berries** and **chopped almonds**.

## Notes

- Use any milk you like — dairy or plant-based both work; the macros above assume semi-skimmed dairy milk.
- Toast the almonds for a minute in a dry pan for extra crunch and aroma.
